Table: Labour
Description: This table contains information about workers involved in the construction project. Each row records a unique labourer or role, their hourly rate, number of hours worked, and the total labour cost calculated from rate and hours.

Table: Material
Description: This table tracks the materials used in construction. It includes the cost per unit of material, quantity used, and total material cost.

Table: Equipment
Description: This table provides data about the equipment rented or used during construction. It captures the daily rental cost and usage duration.
